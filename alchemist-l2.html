Where You Are Now

You’ve put in the hours, maybe even seen flashes of brilliance. But consistency remains elusive. You’re juggling instincts, charts, emotions, and performance — and you know you're close. This is the alchemical grind: turning knowledge into real-world results.

What You Need Most

  • Clear structure without killing creativity
  • Metrics that reflect emotional and technical performance
  • Confidence in your unique rhythm and style

Your Action Guide

Step 1: Create a Weekly Review Ritual
Track P&L, emotions, setups traded, and what felt aligned. Look for patterns in wins and losses beyond the numbers.

Step 2: Define Your “Yes” Trade
Write out the characteristics of a perfect trade for you — setup, context, emotion. Only take those for one week.

Step 3: Build a Personal Dashboard
Use spreadsheets or templates to track not just stats, but your discipline score, clarity, and mindset.

Step 4: Study Your Flow
Notice when you trade best. Morning? After journaling? Use this to build sustainable confidence.
