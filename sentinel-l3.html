Your Action Guide

Step 1: Quantify Your Edge
Start tagging every trade by setup and outcome. Track metrics like win rate, R expectancy, time-in-trade, and setup-specific stats.

Step 2: Build an SOP
Create a Standard Operating Procedure for how you approach each trading session. Include pre-market, execution, and post-review steps.

Step 3: Schedule Innovation
Once per month, allocate time to test new setups or tweak position sizing rules. Keep innovation deliberate, not reactive.

Step 4: Train Like an Athlete
Maintain rituals for sleep, focus, nutrition, journaling. You’re managing yourself as a high-performance system now.
