🔥 Maverick Beginner: Light the Spark

Where You Are Now

You’ve got fire — and that’s rare. As a new Maverick, you feel the pull of the game. The charts excite you. The risk draws you in. But without structure, fire burns fast. You’re here to turn raw instinct into sustainable action.

What You Need Most

  • Channel your intensity into a basic framework
  • Prevent emotional trading before it happens
  • Learn the rhythm of patience and strike timing

Your Action Guide

Step 1: Define Your Rules of Engagement
Write 3 personal trading rules. Example: "I only trade when 2+ conditions align." Stick them near your screen.

Step 2: Use a Trade Countdown
When you see a setup, count to 10 and ask: “What’s my plan?” If you can’t answer, it’s not ready.

Step 3: Focus on Just 1 Market
Whether it’s SPY, BTC, or QQQ — master the rhythm of one symbol. You’ll see patterns faster.

Step 4: Journal the Emotion, Not Just the Trade
After every trade, write how you felt — not just what you saw. This is your edge in disguise.
