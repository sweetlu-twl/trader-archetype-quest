You Are: The Maverick

Bold. Instinctive. Aggressive.

Core Identity

You operate with conviction. You don’t just react — you attack. You excel in high-pressure environments where others hesitate. Risk doesn’t scare you — stagnation does. The market is your arena, and you’re here to play to win.

Tactical Strengths

  • Quick decision-making under pressure
  • Spotting short-term inefficiencies
  • Taking asymmetric risk when opportunity strikes
  • Confidence to act when others freeze

Watch Outs

  • Can overtrade or over-leverage
  • May ignore risk management systems
  • Emotionally reactive after wins/losses
  • May burn out without structure or reflection

Level Up Missions

  • Implement a rule to limit max trades per day or week
  • Track your win rate after impulse trades vs planned trades
  • Study aggressive traders who found discipline: Paul Tudor Jones, Dan Zanger, Steve Cohen
  • Set a weekly pause window to review trades without adrenaline

Inspiration

You thrive on instinct — but your greatest wins come when instinct is paired with restraint. Channel your fire through a narrow path, and it becomes unstoppable. You’re the kind of trader who becomes a legend — if you can survive the early game.
