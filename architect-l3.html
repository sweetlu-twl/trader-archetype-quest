⚙️ Architect Advanced: Optimize & Automate

Where You Are Now

You’ve built your core system. You have a repeatable strategy. Now it’s time to eliminate drag, improve scalability, and sharpen the fine details. You want to trade like a well-oiled machine — but still grow with curiosity.

What You Need Most

  • Refinement of edge through stats and scenario modeling
  • System upgrades — automation, dashboards, routines
  • Optionality — tools to adjust to changing market regimes

Your Action Guide

Step 1: Run a Deep Review
Export and audit your past 3 months of trades. Look for inefficiencies: win rates by setup, days of underperformance, slippage zones.

Step 2: Build or Upgrade Dashboards
Create or refine dashboards that track your KPIs — win %, R-multiple distribution, holding time, entry timing.

Step 3: Explore Select Automation
Whether it’s journaling shortcuts or alert-based entries — begin exploring how tech can reduce friction.

Step 4: Expand Adaptability
Define when your core strategy works best — and when it doesn’t. Build an adjustment plan for those outlier conditions.
