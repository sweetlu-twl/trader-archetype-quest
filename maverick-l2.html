🔥 Maverick Intermediate: Ride the Fire

Where You Are Now

You’ve tasted wins, and felt the sting of overconfidence. You’re no longer new — but consistency is still elusive. You’re discovering that power without control burns out. The next level is about rhythm, awareness, and self-respect.

What You Need Most

  • A reset button after a tilt
  • Pattern recognition across your own behavior
  • Reinforcement of high-quality setups

Your Action Guide

Step 1: Create a Post-Loss Ritual
One minute of breathwork. One journal question: “Did I act with purpose or react from emotion?” This disrupts tilt cycles.

Step 2: Build a Personal Heatmap
Track the conditions of your best and worst trades — time of day, asset, mood, risk size. Look for behavioral patterns.

Step 3: Limit the Volume
Impose a 3-trade-per-day cap. Mavericks thrive on constraints that force quality over chaos.

Step 4: Rate Each Trade Emotionally
Was I calm, charged, rushed, or clear? Add a 1–5 mood score next to each trade in your journal.

Mindset for the Journey

You don’t need more speed — you need more rhythm. Fire can cook or destroy. The Maverick who lasts is the one who stops trying to win *fast* and starts winning *well*.
