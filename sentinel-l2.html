Where You Are Now

You're not a beginner anymore — you've logged screen time, taken some wins and losses, and built some habits. But you're hovering near breakeven. Maybe you second-guess your setups or struggle with hesitation or overtrading. You’re disciplined, but you need refinement.

What You Need Most

  • Clarity on which trades actually fit your style
  • Post-trade analysis to find repeatable patterns
  • One accountability system to track your edge

Your Action Guide

Step 1: Define Your A+ Trade
Review your past 30–50 trades and group your winners by setup type. Look for a pattern of what truly works for you.

Step 2: Use a Playbook
Begin documenting 2–3 of your best setups using screenshots, rules, and emotional notes. This will train consistency.

Step 3: Create a Review Habit
Every week, review your trades by tagging them as "Good Process" or "Mistake." Over time, aim for 80%+ Good Process rate.

Step 4: Cut the Noise
Unfollow accounts, stop strategy-hopping, and narrow your focus. A Sentinel thrives in simplicity.

Mindset for the Journey

This phase isn’t about learning more — it’s about simplifying and executing better. Think like a lighthouse: stable, repetitive, trusted. Repetition is your edge. You’re almost there.
